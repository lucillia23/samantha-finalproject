Pilot Book Pack

Completely new to the world of romance? Wanting to pick up a new series? Browse the books below to find your newest adventure! From contemporary college romances to a high-stakes magical romantasy, here are a few special series I hope you'll enjoy.

The Inheritance Games
Jennifer Lynn Barnes

When a billionaire leaves her his fortune, Avery must outwit his scheming heirs — and survive the deadly puzzles he left behind

Emily Wilde's Encyclopaedia of Faeries
Heather Fawcett

In a remote village, a brilliant scholar cataloging faeries uncovers magic — and a dangerous partnership with a fae prince.

Collide
Bal Khabra

Two strangers crash into each other's lives — and their dark pasts refuse to let go.

Belladonna
Adalyn Grace

After being poisoned, Signa forms a bond with Death itself — and must solve a murder before death claims another.

Caraval
Stephanie Garber

Invited to a legendary game where reality twists and nothing is safe, Scarlett must rescue her kidnapped sister before the final curtain falls.

Powerless
Lauren Roberts

Born without powers in a world of the gifted, she becomes the hunted — and must fight for survival among enemies and traitors.

Once Upon a Broken Heart
Stephanie Garber

Betrayed by love, Evangeline strikes a dangerous deal with the Prince of Hearts — but bargains with fate are never simple.

Throne of Glass
Sarah J. Maas

An assassin wins her freedom by competing for the king — but the kingdom’s real danger lurks beneath the castle.

The Deal
Elle Kennedy

When a hockey player needs tutoring and a smart girl needs fake dating revenge, a simple trade spirals into real feelings.

Icebreaker
Hannah Grace

A figure skater and a hockey star are forced to share the rink — and maybe their hearts — when everything they’ve worked for is on the line.

Daughter of Smoke and Bone
Laini Taylor

A blue-haired artist raised by monsters discovers her forgotten past — and a forbidden love that could start a war.

Twisted Love
Ana Huang

Haunted by his past, Alex swore to protect Ava — but the secrets between them could destroy everything they build.

Fourth Wing
Rebecca Yarros

Forced into a brutal dragon rider academy, Violet must survive impossible trials — or be burned alive trying.

A Court of Thorns and Roses
Sarah J. Maas

To save her family, Feyre kills a faerie wolf — only to be dragged into a deadly world of magic, curses, and unexpected love.

Lovelight Farms
B.K. Borison

Stella fakes a romance with her best friend to save her failing Christmas tree farm — but the feelings quickly become all too real.
